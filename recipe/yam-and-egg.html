Indomie Noodles

Description

This is my best delicacy personally.

It is the favourite of so many people as well.

Ingredients

  • Yam
  • Eggs
  • Onions
  • Sugar
  • Vegetable Oil

Steps

  1. Slice yam into pieces of your of your choice.
  2. Put in water and add little sugar to cook for 20 minutes
  3. Prepare 3-4 eggs to fry after heating the vegetable oil.
  4. Prepare the egg and serve together with the yam.
