Indomie Noodles

Description

This is a relatively easy recipe to prepare.

It is most times prepared for children. However, grownups like it as well.

Ingredients

  • Indomie
  • Pepper
  • Onions
  • Vegetables
  • Chicken

Steps

  1. Boil water till it is hot for 2-3 minutes.
  2. Put the noodles in the water to cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Spray the ingredients on it and stir for one minute.
